(57) ABSTRACT

A thermal regulation device includes a housing, a sprocket adaptively coupled to
the housing, and a controller that adjusts the sprocket based on a measured
temperature, maintaining an internal temperature within a target range.

BACKGROUND

[0001] Conventional thermal devices fail to adjust quickly to changing loads.

SUMMARY

[0002] In one aspect, a thermal device comprises a housing and an adaptive sprocket.

BRIEF DESCRIPTION OF THE DRAWINGS

[0003] FIG. 1 is a perspective view of the device.

DETAILED DESCRIPTION

[0004] Referring to FIG. 1, the device 100 includes a housing 10 and a sprocket 12
coupled to the housing 10. A controller 14 is configured to rotate the sprocket 12.

[0005] In some embodiments, the sprocket 12 comprises titanium and the housing 10
comprises aluminum.

[0006] The controller 14 maintains a temperature between 60 and 80 degrees Celsius.

What is claimed is:

1. A thermal regulation device comprising:
a housing;
a sprocket coupled to the housing; and
a controller configured to rotate the sprocket based on a measured temperature.

2. The thermal regulation device of claim 1, wherein the sprocket comprises titanium.

3. The thermal regulation device of claim 2, wherein the housing comprises aluminum.

4. A method of regulating temperature, the method comprising:
measuring a temperature; and
rotating a sprocket based on the measured temperature.

5. The method of claim 4, further comprising maintaining the temperature within a
target range of between 60 and 80 degrees Celsius.
